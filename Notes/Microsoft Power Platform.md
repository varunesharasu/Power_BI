**Power BI Ecosystem**



1\. Power BI Desktop – Data modeling, transformation, and report development.

2\. Power BI Service – Report publishing, sharing, and collaboration.

3\. Power BI Mobile App – Access dashboards and reports on mobile devices.

4\. Microsoft Fabric – Unified analytics platform integrating data engineering, data science, data warehousing, and business intelligence.

5\. Azure – Cloud services for data storage, processing, and analytics.

6\. Power Automate – Workflow automation and business process integration.
